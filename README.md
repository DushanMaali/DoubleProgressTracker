# DoubleProgressTracker

**DoubleProgressTracker** is an Android application designed to help users follow the **Double Progression Method** for strength training. This method focuses on gradually increasing reps and weights to maximize muscle growth and strength gains.

## ðŸ‹ï¸â€â™‚ï¸ Double Progression Method Explained

The Double Progression Method is a training approach where you:

1. Perform sets of an exercise aiming for a **target rep range**.
2. Increase the weight when you reach the **upper rep limit** for all sets.
3. Gradually progress over time to build strength and muscle effectively.

This app automates tracking and helps you **stay consistent with your progression**.
